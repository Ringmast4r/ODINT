## Email Spoofing Impact

With no DMARC, attackers can:
- Spoof emails from @mae.gouv.ht (foreign ministry)
- Spoof emails from @dgi.gouv.ht (tax authority)
- Spoof emails from @primature.gouv.ht (prime minister)
- Spoof emails from @mjsp.gouv.ht (justice ministry)

This could be used for:
- Phishing campaigns
- Executive impersonation
- Regulatory fraud
- Financial transfers
- Policy impersonation

## Compliance Notes

DMARC is foundational email security, not optional.
Recommended by: CISA, NSA, NIST, Microsoft, Google, SANS
Industry standard: Implement p=reject for all organizational email
